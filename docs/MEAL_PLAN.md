# Rakshith's Meal Plan — Fat Loss (high-protein, home-cooked)

> **Daily targets: ~1,975 kcal · 160 g protein · ~58 g fat · ~200 g carbs · 30 g+ fibre.**
> Built from your TDEE (~2,475 kcal, verified two ways) minus a 500 kcal deficit. Non-veg, rice-first, home-cooked only — pulled from your existing recomp plan.
>
> **How to read this:** every meal and snack has **two options (A & B)** that play the same role, so you can rotate by mood/stock without touching your macros. Each has ingredients, method, and nutrition. Flex rules below tell you how to run **3 meals + 1 snack**, **3 meals no snack**, or **4 meals** — all landing on the same daily total.

## Flex rules — same daily total, three ways

**► 3 meals + 1 snack (base plan):**
Banana + Breakfast + Lunch + Snack + Dinner. Verified default (A/A/A + Dinner A):

| | kcal | Protein | Fat | Carbs |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Banana + coffee | 105 | 1 | 0 | 27 |
| Breakfast A | 550 | 51 | 15 | 53 |
| Lunch A | 615 | 57 | 13 | 65 |
| Snack A | 270 | 27 | 5 | 28 |
| Dinner A | 410 | 22 | 20 | 37 |
| **TOTAL** | **~1,950** | **158 g** | **53 g** | **210 g** ✓ |

**► 3 meals, no snack:**
Skip the snack and add its protein back: put **1 scoop whey (in milk or water) + a fruit into breakfast or dinner**. That replaces the ~27 g protein / ~250 kcal you dropped. Nothing else changes.

**► 4 meals:**
Add **Meal 4** and pull ~250 kcal out to stay level — easiest is **drop the snack and use Meal 4 instead**, or keep the snack and **trim 150 g of rice across lunch + dinner**. Either way the day still lands ~1,975 / 160.

> **Non-negotiables:** hit protein (155–165 g) and total calories every day. Fat/carb split can flex. If you fall short on protein, a whey scoop is always your safety net.

## ⚡ Time-crunch fallback — full no-cook / assembly-only day

Same targets (~1,975 kcal · ~160 g protein), **zero real cooking.** Keep these stocked: whey, Greek yogurt/curd, tuna tins, a tray of pre-boiled eggs, roasted chana, ready-to-eat dal pouch (MTR/Tasty Bite), microwave/pre-cooked rice, brown bread, fruit, chia.

*Short on protein?* Add ½–1 scoop whey anywhere to top up to 160 g.
**One-time prep that makes this work:** boil a full tray of eggs (lasts 5 days), keep 3–4 tuna tins and 2 dal pouches in the cupboard, and soak overnight oats before bed.

## Adjusting over time

Weigh most mornings, track the **7-day average**. Aim to lose **0.3–0.5 kg/week**.
- In range → change nothing.
- Not moving after 2–3 weeks → **add ~1,500 steps/day first**, or trim 150 kcal (carbs/fat, keep protein at 160). One lever at a time.
- Losing >0.7 kg/week or strength dropping → add ~150 kcal back. Faster costs muscle.
